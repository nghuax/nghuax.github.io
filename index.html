01

Pause before
you scroll.

I'm An — a designer who treats every pixel like editorial copy. My work lives between research and rhythm: slow, considered, and curious about how people actually read an interface.

For three years I've helped early-stage brands and non-profits turn messy ideas into calm, confident products. I care about typography, generous whitespace, and systems that outlive the designer who made them.
