About the portfolio.

An Thai · 1996

Vietnamese coffee · 30+ export markets

Portfolio subject — An Thai, Vietnamese coffee company.

This e-portfolio collects four portfolio entries completed for MKT3039 Digital Marketing, each applied to An Thai — a Vietnamese coffee company founded in 1996 that exports to more than thirty countries (An Thai Group, n.d.).

Entry 1 · The Ladder of Engagement introduces the engagement ladder (Mergel, 2014) and examines how An Thai's audiences move across low, mid and high levels of participation. The chapter diagnoses where the brand is strong (awareness, information) and where it is under-built (advocacy, co-creation).

Entry 2 · Social Media Evaluation evaluates Facebook — Vietnam's leading social platform, with 76.2 million users in 2025 (DataReportal, 2025a) — as a marketing channel for An Thai using the RACE framework (Chaffey, 2023). Each RACE stage is analysed with examples drawn from An Thai's current activity.

Entry 3 · Website Checklist sets out four criteria for a good e-commerce website — functional content, ease of use, aesthetic design and on-page SEO — builds a scoring checklist, and audits the AnTháiCafé website against it (AnTháiCafé, n.d.).

Entry 4 · Trend Analysis examines social commerce — the most commercially relevant digital trend for An Thai in a Vietnamese market estimated at US$32 billion (VECOM, 2025) — and shows how brands are using it through two applications: social discovery and Messenger-led commerce.

The portfolio closes with a Harvard-referenced bibliography. All in-text citations follow the Harvard convention.
